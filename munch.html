MunchMate

Deliverables

UX Research, UX/UI Design, Mobile Design

MunchMate is a mobile-first dating app designed for food lovers seeking romantic, friendly, or collaborative connections through shared cravings and culinary chemistry. It blends personality, lifestyle, and foodie identity into a playful experience where finding a match is as fun as finding your next bite.


Create a friendly, inclusive platform that connects people based on food preferences, lifestyle, and vibe — whether for dating or friendship.
