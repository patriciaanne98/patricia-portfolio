Echoes of Silence

Project

Echoes of Silence

Deliverables

UX Research, UI Kit, Mobile App Design

Echoes of Silence is an AI-powered expression tool designed to empower non-verbal individuals through emotionally intuitive communication. Blending adaptive technology with a deeply human-centered interface, the app transforms typed words, spoken thoughts, and visual cues into meaningful emotional expression.


From journey mapping to high-fidelity UI, the full experience was crafted to feel safe, supportive, and personal — helping users track growth, explore emotion, and express themselves in ways that go beyond language. The result is a seamless and sensitive design that reimagines communication for those who’ve long been unheard.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Echoes of Silence is a self-expression app designed for people who struggle with emotional communication. By combining journaling, conversational AI, and mood tracking, it helps users release thoughts, navigate complex emotions, and reflect through a safe, supportive interface.

PROBLEM

Many people, especially young adults, find it difficult to express emotions or articulate inner thoughts, leading to emotional bottling, stress, and social withdrawal. Mental health tools often feel clinical or overwhelming—there’s a need for an emotionally intuitive, low-pressure alternative.

Objectives

  • Understand what prevents people from expressing emotions or journaling regularly
  • Design an experience that feels safe, intuitive, and emotionally supportive
  • Make prompts, responses, and mood-tracking features easy to access and use
  • Add human-centered cues — relatable prompts, gentle AI responses, and reflection tools
  • Create a calming, mobile-friendly interface that encourages consistent emotional check-ins

Goal

To design a gentle, AI-powered space that helps users talk through their emotions without judgment, and to give them personalized insights and resources through mood-based prompts and feedback.
