TANGÉ is a vitamin C wellness supplement brand designed to bring playful energy and elevated simplicity to daily self-care. I created a full visual identity system including logo, packaging, color palette, and digital assets tailored for a modern wellness audience.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Supplements are personal. For some, they’re about energy and immunity. For others, they’re a ritual of care — a pause in the chaos. But the truth is: most supplement brands forget the human. I set out to design TANGÉ — a vitamin C gummy brand and online store that doesn’t just promise wellness, but earns trust through vibrant design, transparent storytelling, and joyful interaction. From credibility to crave-ability, I reimagined how a supplement could look, feel, and convert.

PROBLEM

The supplement space is packed with brands using flashy claims and hard-to-read labels. Many people feel unsure about what they're taking or if it even works. TANGÉ set out to change that by showing — not just telling — what’s inside and why it matters.


We needed to create a brand that felt honest, friendly, and reliable — with real proof, not marketing fluff.

Objectives

  • Understand what motivates people to choose one supplement over another
  • Design a brand that feels premium, modern, and easy to trust
  • Make ingredients, benefits, and test results super clear
  • Add social proof — real reviews, press features, and test results
  • Create a mobile-friendly shop that’s clean, simple, and fun to explore

Goal

How might we make taking a vitamin feel less like a chore — and more like an act of self-care that earns your trust?
