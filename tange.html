TANGÉ is a vitamin C wellness supplement brand designed to bring playful energy and elevated simplicity to daily self-care. I created a full visual identity system including logo, packaging, color palette, and digital assets tailored for a modern wellness audience.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Supplements are personal. For some, they’re about energy and immunity. For others, they’re a ritual of care — a pause in the chaos. But the truth is: most supplement brands forget the human. We set out to design TANGÉ — a vitamin C gummy brand and online store that doesn’t just promise wellness, but earns trust through vibrant design, transparent storytelling, and joyful interaction. From credibility to crave-ability, we reimagined how a supplement could look, feel, and convert.

PROBLEM

TANGÉ was created in response to a growing consumer demand for supplements that don’t just claim to be clean — but prove it. The wellness space is crowded with brands that rely on buzzwords, unverified health claims, and bland, medical packaging. Consumers are left skeptical, uninspired, and overwhelmed.


TANGÉ set out to flip the narrative: what if taking a supplement felt joyful, trustworthy, and even... beautiful?


I designed a brand and digital experience that celebrates daily rituals with clear ingredient visuals, third-party test results, and editorial-level storytelling. The challenge was to build an identity and online store that were as credible as they were craveable.

Objectives

  • Conduct user research to understand the motivations and hesitations of vitamin C supplement shoppers
  • Create a digital-first brand identity that feels premium, fun, and human
  • Design an e-commerce experience that increases conversion through trust-building visuals and ingredient transparency
  • Integrate test results, press mentions (CNN, Forbes, NYT), and user reviews without overwhelming the product story
  • Optimize for both desktop and mobile with playful micro-interactions and clear calls to action

Goal

How might we design a supplement shopping experience that builds trust through clarity, design, and joy — all while standing out in a saturated, skeptical market?

User Interviews

Needs

  • Clear breakdown of ingredients and their purpose
  • Assurance that the supplement is backed by science and tested by third parties
  • An easy way to understand dosage, frequency, and benefits
  • Confidence that what they’re buying actually works

Wants

  • Packaging that looks aesthetic enough to keep on a shelf or desk
  • A shopping experience that’s fast, beautiful, and doesn’t feel like a pharmacy
  • Credible endorsements from real people and real publications (not bots or fake reviews)
  • A simple subscription or refill option without commitment stress

Desires

  • To feel like they’re making a better choice — for their body and their lifestyle
  • To see visual proof: lab results, glowing testimonials, social proof that feels real
  • To trust a wellness brand enough to recommend it to a friend
  • o connect their routine with a bigger purpose — like glowing skin, immunity, or mental clarity
  • To escape the sea of sterile, orange pill bottles and feel part of a lifestyle movement
