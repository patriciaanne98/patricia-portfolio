TANGÉ

Project

TANGÉ

Role

UX/UI Designer+ Brand Strategist

Deliverables

Branding, Illustrations, UX/UI Design

Tools

Figma, Sketch, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

A vitamin C supplement brand designed to reduce buyer hesitation through human-centered design, ingredient transparency, and digital trust-building. I led UX research, product strategy, wireframes, visual identity, and UI for a clean, mobile-first wellness e-commerce experience.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Supplements are personal. For some, they’re about energy and immunity. For others, they’re a ritual of care — a pause in the chaos. But the truth is: most supplement brands forget the human. I set out to design TANGÉ — a vitamin C gummy brand and online store that doesn’t just promise wellness, but earns trust through vibrant design, transparent storytelling, and joyful interaction. From credibility to crave-ability, I reimagined how a supplement could look, feel, and convert.

CHALLENGE

Shoppers are overwhelmed by vague wellness claims, clinical packaging, and unclear ingredients. Many don’t trust what they’re buying. TANGÉ aimed to build a transparent, feel-good supplement experience grounded in clarity, trust signals, and modern UX — not marketing hype.

SUCCESS CRITERIA

  • Uncover what drives supplement trust and hesitation
  • Design a premium brand experience users can believe in
  • Prioritize ingredient clarity and benefit communication
  • Use social proof to build reputation and credibility
  • Optimize for mobile-first usability and low-friction shopping

Goal

How might we transform supplement shopping from confusing and clinical to transparent, joyful, and trustworthy?

From Skepticism to Trust: Designing a Wellness Brand That Feels Honest

In today’s oversaturated supplement market, users are often met with vague claims, crowded packaging, and doubt. TANGÉ was my opportunity to rethink the experience from a place of transparency, emotional clarity, and clean design.


I wasn’t just designing a store — I was designing trust.


Through visual storytelling, clear ingredient breakdowns, and subtle UX decisions like test result callouts and calming colors, I helped transform supplement shopping from something clinical and skeptical into something users described as “simple,” “clean,” and “refreshing.”


This approach reflects how we can build confidence in digital health, pharma products, or wellness tools by listening to what users need emotionally — not just clinically.
